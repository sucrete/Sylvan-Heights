Historic Greens.
Exceptional Service.

Experience the finest golf in the Western Pennsylvania region at Sylvan Heights Golf Course. Our facility seamlessly combines the region’s natural beauty with a fun, challenging course design and exceptional player services. Our greens have multiple tee distances to accommodate players of all skill and age levels.

Proudly managed by the city’s Department of Parks and Recreation, Sylvan Heights stands as one of the oldest municipal public courses in the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania. Our fairways are steeped in history, having hosted legendary figures like Arnold Palmer during the 1948 West Penn Amateur. Come walk the historic greens in the footsteps of champions!
